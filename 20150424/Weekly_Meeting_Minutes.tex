\section {Things to note}
\begin{enumerate}
\item When we learn an algorithm, we should try to use it first and then adjust the parameters to analyze the results, only in this way can we understand the algorithm and know its strengths and weaknesses.
\item The more you do, the more you will learn.
\item If you spend time on something, you must learn from it, or you will be wasting your time.
\item It's hard to learn all by yourself without checking out how far you have been on your research direction.
\item When we give a report, we should focus on the main idea of the report and cut the crap.
\end{enumerate}
